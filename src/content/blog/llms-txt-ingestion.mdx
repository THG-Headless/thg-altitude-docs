# LLM Friendly Documentation

AI agents are only as powerful as the data they can access, and now, thanks to Altitude’s support for llms.txt, you can seamlessly integrate our technical documentation into your AI workflows.

## What is llms.txt?

The llms.txt file is a standardized way for websites to make their content available to AI models and agents. Much like robots.txt guides search engine crawlers, llms.txt provides AI systems with structured instructions on how to access and utilize documentation effectively. This means AI agents can now retrieve, process, and apply Altitude’s technical resources with greater accuracy and efficiency.

## Why Does This Matter?

*Seamless AI Integration* – Your AI tools can now pull from Altitude’s latest documentation without manual scraping or outdated references.

*Enhanced AI Performance* – More accurate and up-to-date technical knowledge leads to better responses and problem-solving capabilities.

*Developer Friendly* – Whether you're integrating an LLM-powered chatbot or an internal AI assistant, getting started with Altitude’s llms.txt makes documentation ingestion effortless.

## How to Use It

Simply configure your agent to ingest our llms.txt into your AI discussions or your ingestion workflows and and you’ll have access to structured and relevant documentation.
