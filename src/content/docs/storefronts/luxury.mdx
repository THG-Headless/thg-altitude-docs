# Luxury Storefront

The Luxury Storefront is a sophisticated white-label solution built on Altitude, crafted specifically for luxury and premium retail brands. It combines elegant design capabilities with high-end e-commerce features to deliver exceptional shopping experiences.

## Key Features

- **Premium UX**: Designed for high-end retail experiences
- **VIP Pricing**: Exclusive pricing tiers for premium customers
- **Sponsored Ads**: Integrated Criteo support for premium product placement
- **Provenance**: Built-in support for product authenticity and origin tracking

## Platform Capabilities

The Luxury Storefront includes a comprehensive set of features designed for premium retail:

### Core Commerce
- Sophisticated product discovery
- Integrated reviews and ratings
- Premium wishlist functionality
- Advanced loyalty program support

### Internationalization
- Multi-property support for global luxury brands
- Subsite capabilities for market-specific experiences
- International domain support (GTLD)

### Marketing & Analytics
- Advanced A/B testing
- Sophisticated campaign segmentation
- Premium email marketing integration
- Comprehensive analytics suite

- OneTrust consent management
- WordPress integration through Tesseract
- Dynamic Yield personalization (Work in Progress)
